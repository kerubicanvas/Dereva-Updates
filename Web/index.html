Welcome to Dereva Academy!
Your Guide to Mastering your NTSA Driving Test

Dereva Academy prepares learners for driving success in Kenya and other left-side driving countries, following NTSA guidelines.

Whether you're gearing up for your NTSA driving test or polishing your skills, Dereva Academy is here to support you on your journey.
We offer a fun and interactive way to practice navigation on the model town and traffic signs in a virtual enviroment.

Here's what you can expect with Dereva Academy:

  • Become a confident driver with anytime, anywhere practice on our mobile app.
  • Access and download relevant driver education documents.
  • Test your understanding of traffic signs with fun quizzes.

  • Challenge your friends and climb the leaderboard to prove your mastery!
